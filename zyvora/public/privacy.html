Privacy Policy

Effective: 16 July 2026 · ZYVORA is operated by ZYFORA.

1. What we collect

  • Account data: your email address and authentication records.
  • Business Memory: the business records you choose to enter (orders, products, expenses, customer contacts, notes). In local device mode this never leaves your browser.
  • Billing data: handled by Stripe. We store only your subscription status and Stripe identifiers — never card numbers.
  • Error reports: if the app crashes, we receive the technical error (message, stack trace, browser version) so we can fix it. Error reports never include your business records.

2. What we do with it

We use your data to run the service, sync it across your devices, and support you. That's it. We do not sell it, rent it, profile you for advertising, or train public AI models on it.

3. AI features

When you ask ZYVORA a question in account mode, a factual summary of your business figures is sent to our AI provider (currently Groq) to generate the answer, over an encrypted connection via our server. It is used to answer your question, not to build advertising profiles.

4. Messaging features

If you send WhatsApp/SMS messages to your customers, the phone number and message text pass through our server to Twilio for delivery. You are the controller of your customers' data; message them lawfully.

5. Where data lives

Cloud data is stored with Supabase (Postgres) and protected by row-level security: each workspace's data is only readable by its members. Access to your data by us is limited to what operating and supporting the service requires.

6. Your rights

  • Export: one-click export of your entire Business Memory, anytime.
  • Deletion: ask us to delete your account and cloud data, and we will — permanently. (Export first; deletion is not reversible.)
  • Access/correction: your data is visible in the app itself; contact us for anything beyond that.

7. Cookies

ZYVORA uses only what it needs to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. No third-party advertising trackers.

8. Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll notify you in the app or by email before it takes effect.
