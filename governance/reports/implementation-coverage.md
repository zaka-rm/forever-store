## Reading of the result

The MVP covers **~31% of the full 12-capability enterprise platform by feature count**, but the coverage is *deliberately concentrated* where the product's value lives:

- **Strong (50–69%): the decision core** — Decision Center, Business Memory, AI Engine (as rules), Inventory. This is the heart of a Decision Operating System, and it's the most complete.
- **Moderate (31–38%): the commerce/finance operational layer** — Finance, CRM, Analytics have their MVP surface but not enterprise depth (no double-entry accounting, no CRM pipelines, no governed metric registry).
- **Thin/absent (0–31%): enterprise breadth** — Identity/permissions (the multi-user gap), Documents, Notifications, HR, Manufacturing.

This matches the strategy: build the decision loop and the real Naturaloe commerce flow first, defer enterprise breadth. The single highest-leverage gap for real daily use is **IAM (multi-user/permissions)** at 31%.
